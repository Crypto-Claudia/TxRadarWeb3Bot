# Privacy Policy for TxRadar Bot

**Effective Date:** June 26, 2026

TxRadar ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the TxRadar Telegram Bot (the "Bot").

By using the Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide our real-time Ethereum transaction alert services, we collect only the minimum necessary information:

* **Telegram User Data**: We collect your Telegram User ID, Chat ID, and Username. This is required to route transaction alerts directly to your Telegram chat and to recognize you when you interact with the Bot.
* **Monitored Addresses and Labels**: We store the public Ethereum addresses (smart contracts or wallets) that you register via the `/add` command, along with any custom labels you voluntarily assign to them.
* **Command Usage Data**: We may log basic command interactions (e.g., when you call `/list` or `/help`) to monitor bot performance and debug issues.

### 🚫 What We NEVER Collect

* **Private Keys or Seed Phrases**: We will **NEVER** ask for, collect, or store your private keys, seed phrases, or passwords. Your private keys should never be shared with anyone.
* **Personal Identity Information**: We do not collect your real name, physical address, email address, phone number, or any financial/payment card information.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

* To monitor the Ethereum blockchain in real-time for transactions related to your registered addresses.
* To deliver instant deposit and withdrawal alerts to your Telegram chat.
* To manage and display your personal watchlist within the Bot (via the `/list` command).
* To maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the Bot's performance and stability.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

* **Security**: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your Telegram Chat ID and monitored address list from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
* **Public Blockchain Data**: Please note that all transactions, wallet balances, and smart contract activities on the Ethereum mainnet are public by design. TxRadar only tracks and formats this publicly available on-chain data; we do not have control over blockchain public records.

---

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion (Your Rights)

You have full control over your data:

* **Deleting Monitored Addresses**: You can stop monitoring and delete any registered address and its associated label at any time by using the `/remove <address>` command inside the Bot.
* **Full Account Deletion**: If you wish to delete all of your data from our database, you can block/stop the Bot. Alternatively, you can contact us directly (see Contact section below), and we will manually purge your data (Chat ID and watchlist) within 30 days of the request.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

* **Telegram Infrastructure**: The Bot operates on the Telegram Messenger platform. Your use of Telegram is subject to Telegram's own Privacy Policy.
* **Blockchain Node Providers**: We utilize third-party RPC node providers (such as Infura, Alchemy, or QuickNode) to fetch public blockchain data. No personal data or Telegram IDs are shared with these node providers.

---

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and/or sending a notification through the Bot. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
